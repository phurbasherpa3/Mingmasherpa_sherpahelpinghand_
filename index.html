Who We Are

About Sherpa Helping Hand

Based in the heart of Okhaldhunga, Nepal, Sherpa Helping Hand believes every child deserves access to quality education — regardless of their circumstances.

We work hand in hand with local communities to remove barriers to learning. From funding scholarships for deserving students to supplying books and stationery, and constructing schools in underserved areas, our mission is rooted in compassion, accountability, and lasting impact.

Education is the most powerful tool for change. With your support, we continue to build brighter futures for children across Okhaldhunga and beyond.

Okhaldhunga, Nepal

Our Vision

A Nepal where every child has the resources and opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive.

Our Mission

To provide scholarships, educational materials, and school infrastructure to underserved communities in Okhaldhunga.

What We Do

Our Programs

Three pillars of support that create lasting change in the lives of students and communities.

01

Student Scholarships

We identify and support deserving students who demonstrate academic promise but lack financial means. Our scholarships cover tuition, fees, and related educational expenses so talented young minds can continue their studies without worry.

02

Books & Stationery

Access to textbooks, notebooks, pens, and other essential supplies should never be a barrier to learning. We distribute quality educational materials directly to students and schools across Okhaldhunga.

03

School Construction

We build and renovate schools in remote areas of Okhaldhunga where educational infrastructure is lacking. Safe, well-equipped classrooms give children a proper environment to learn and succeed.

We also support monastery build and renovation projects that strengthen the local community and preserve cultural heritage.
